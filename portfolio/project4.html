✍️ Trying something new

Restaurant branding and menu designing has always been something I've been interested in, so I decided to rebrand one of my favorite restaurants, Tselogs, for fun. This Filipinx restaurant is inspired by the suffix -silog, which means to add an egg (itlog) and fried rice (sinangag) to whatever main dish (ulam) you want. Spamsilog (Spam, fried rice, and egg) was one of my favorite breakfast meals growing up, so going to Tselogs with friends when I'm in SF (my new home away from home) always reminds me of my childhood.

Branding

🌟 Branding Intentions: Tselogs' current branding is minimal and traditional. I wanted to create a more playful and modern atmosphere, while maintaining their minimalistic style through additional colors, illustrations, and casual typeface.

Assets

Reflection

Rebranding Tselogs' was a great first branding experience, and I was able to further enhance my Illustrator skills with this project while learning more about brand purposes and styles within the food industry. My branding process emphasized the intersection of marketing and design, and proved to me how important target marketing and thinking of your audience is when it comes to brand identity.

Moving forward, I plan to work on other projects involving brand design and hope to explore other brand styles for more products that need a more established brand identity.
